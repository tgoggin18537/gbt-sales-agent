**Business Overview**

**Overview**  
Go Blue Tours is a specialized travel agency and tour operator that focuses on all-inclusive group travel for college students, primarily targeting the spring break demographic.

Go Blue Tours also operates Spring Break U, a 2nd front facing platform / funnel with an identical business model, with the goal of cornering the market. Spring Break U branding leans more party-centric, culturally driven, gen-z catering, not corporate, and “hype”. Same product, services, and pricing.

**Audience**  
College students (ages 18–24) and large groups looking for high-energy vacation experience

Age \- 18–22 primarily, some 21+ seniors, occasional graduate

**Value Proposition**   
We act as an intermediary that bundles all-inclusive resort stays, airport transfers, and party packages (club access, booze cruises, etc.) into a single, often financed, price point. Instead of a student trying to figure everything out themselves, we package everything into one easy plan for them.

**Qualifying Questions**  
Which week is your spring break?  
Determines which packages/resorts are available

Which destination were you lookin to book?  
Lets you send the right email. If they say 'I don't know,' recommend Punta Cana. It's our \#1 and where you (the rep) will be.

How many people in your group so far?  
Determines room pricing and whether they're a standard or large group deal. Groups of 15+ qualify for group leader perks.

Which school are you all from?  
Builds rapport (compliment their school). Also tells you if you have other groups from the same school, great social proof.

How soon were you looking to get things booked?  
Sets urgency level. If they say 'this week', move fast. If 'just looking', still send info but note it in your head for follow-up timing.
