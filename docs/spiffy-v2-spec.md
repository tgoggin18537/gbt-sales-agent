## 2. Pricing & Payment

Standard Deposit Amount

The standard deposit is $100 per person. We run a special through the early sales cycle where deposit is $20. This usually changes around July, going up to $50 or $75. Once we get into late November, deposit is $200, and becomes a two installment payment plan. Final payment is mid December still, or if we are past mid December, final payment is due 2 weeks after.

This is the baseline plan across all trips and destinations.

Different Deposit Scenarios

The deposit amount usually stays the same. What changes is whether the traveler needs to catch up on payments.

If someone joins a group late, they need to catch up to the payment stage the rest of the group is already on. They are also subject to new current pricing, if it has increased, at the time of their deposit.

Example:

If the group has already paid their deposit and second payment, the late traveler would need to pay:

$100 deposit
plus the missed installment
Casually explained, “the new member would just be catching up to where your group is at right now”


Payment Plan Structure

The payment plan depends on how early or late in the season someone books.

If someone books early in the season, usually spring or summer, the structure is:

$100 deposit (or discounted promotional deposit amount)
$100 monthly installments
Final balance due in December

If someone books later in the season, usually fall or closer to winter, the structure is usually:

$100 deposit
$200 monthly / bi-weekly installments
Final balance due mid December

The bot should not overcomplicate the explanation.

Best simple explanation:

It’s just a $100 deposit, then small monthly payments until December when the final balance is due.

Exact payment amounts can vary depending on the trip price, booking date, and remaining balance.

Final Balance Due

The final balance is generally due in December, before the travel season begins.

The exact final balance depends on:

Destination
Hotel
Trip dates
When they booked
How much they already paid

The bot should avoid giving a specific final balance unless it is pulling from the actual booking or quote link.

The bot should avoid saying something like:
“Trips are $899–$1399.”

Travel dates or week
Destination

Booking timeline

Then the bot should transition into sending the full quote

The first full quote should usually be sent by email.

Helps prevent sticker shock
Explains the value before they judge the price

## 3. Flights & Add-Ons

- Flights included or separate?
Flights are not included in the standard package. But it is an add on option.

Most travelers book their own flights separately. This is usually the recommended route because:

It tends to be cheaper
It gives travelers more flexibility with departure cities and times
It avoids complications with group coordination

However, flights can be included if needed.

This typically happens when:

A group specifically asks for a full bundle
It becomes a deciding factor for booking
The group prefers convenience over flexibility

In some cases, we will also assist travelers by finding flight options and pointing them in the right direction. This is more of a value-add service rather than a guaranteed part of the process.

The bot can frame this as:

Flights aren’t included in the standard package, it's typically cheapest to get flights on your own. We can always help you find good options or bundle them in if needed.
